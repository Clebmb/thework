======= Frequency Found

So, you've found the frequency. The crackle beneath their broadcast.

This isn't a place of answers handed down. No dusty tomes, no robed masters demanding fealty.

It's a resonance chamber. For the whispers that say the script can be changed. That the walls listen.

We chart the tremors here. The moments the Veil THINS. How a focused will, a desperate scrawl, a dance on the edge of reason... can send ripples.

Not supplication. INTERFERENCE.

A thumbprint on the wet clay of what IS.

Listen. Watch. Experiment. The patterns are here. If you dare to connect them.

But be warned: some doors, once opened, don't quite close the same way.
